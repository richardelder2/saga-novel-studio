# Gemini Native TTS Voice Catalog

Welcome to the CoAuthor Voice Catalog! This reference guide compiles the available prebuilt voices for the Gemini Native Text-to-Speech API (`gemini-3.1-flash-tts-preview` and `gemini-2.0-flash`). Use these voice names in your character profiles to map high-quality, expressive voices to specific characters.

## 🎭 Detailed Voice Profiles

### Male Voices

#### 🌌 Charon
*   **Gender:** Male
*   **Pitch:** Deep
*   **Vibe:** Clear, professional, and comforting.
*   **Best For:** The Narrator. It holds a pleasant, steady pacing suitable for descriptive text and setting scenes. Also fits mature, experienced characters who speak with calm authority.

#### 🐺 Fenrir
*   **Gender:** Male
*   **Pitch:** Lower-Mid
*   **Vibe:** Warm, rich, and highly expressive.
*   **Best For:** Protagonists, active main characters, or intense dialogue. Its rich character makes emotional spikes and dramatic statements sound authentic.

#### 🧚 Puck
*   **Gender:** Male
*   **Pitch:** Mid-Range
*   **Vibe:** Energetic, bright, and enthusiastic.
*   **Best For:** Friendly sidekicks, fast-talkers, or younger characters. It maintains high engagement and adds a lighter, conversational touch.

#### 🕰️ Autonoe
*   **Gender:** Male
*   **Pitch:** Deeper / Resonant
*   **Vibe:** Mature, slow-paced, and highly thoughtful.
*   **Best For:** Reclusive mentors, older figures, wizards, or complex anti-heroes who weigh their words carefully.

#### 👑 Zubenelgenubi
*   **Gender:** Male
*   **Pitch:** Very Deep / Resonant
*   **Vibe:** Unwavering, formal, and authoritative.
*   **Best For:** Primary antagonists, high-ranking military commanders, or corporate oligarchs. It has a commanding presence.

### Female Voices

#### 🎵 Aoede
*   **Gender:** Female
*   **Pitch:** Mid-Range
*   **Vibe:** Breezy, intelligent, dynamic, and natural.
*   **Best For:** Primary female protagonists or an alternative female Narrator. Excellent flow, emotional variation, and realistic pacing.

#### 🛡️ Leda
*   **Gender:** Female
*   **Pitch:** Mid-Range
*   **Vibe:** Composed, serious, professional, and authoritative.
*   **Best For:** Smart scientists, detectives, rulers, or high-intelligence specialists. Speaks with precision and clarity.

#### ⚡ Kore
*   **Gender:** Female
*   **Pitch:** Mid-High
*   **Vibe:** Confident, firm, and enthusiastic.
*   **Best For:** Rebels, action-oriented heroines, or energetic companions. It has a slight edge that projects strength.

#### 🍃 Zephyr
*   **Gender:** Female
*   **Pitch:** Mid-Range
*   **Vibe:** Bright, upbeat, and perky.
*   **Best For:** Friendly secondary characters, eager assistants, or highly optimistic individuals.

#### 🌸 Despina
*   **Gender:** Female
*   **Pitch:** Mid-Range
*   **Vibe:** Smooth, warm, and inviting.
*   **Best For:** Healers, loving parental figures, or peaceful diplomats. It has a soft, approachable texture.

## ⚙️ How to Apply in Character Profiles

Simply add the **Gender** and **Voice Assignment** fields to your character markdown files in `00_Story_Bible/characters/`. For example:

```markdown
# character_name.md

**Role in Story:** Protagonist
**Gender:** Male
**Voice Assignment:** Fenrir
```
