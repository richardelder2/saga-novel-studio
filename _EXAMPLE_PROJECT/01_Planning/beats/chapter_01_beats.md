# Beats: Chapter 1 - The Synthetic Sludge

**Goal:** Arthur wants to survive the morning rush at "Joe's Fuel Station" without spilling any Nutri-Gloop on a cyborg.

**Conflict:** The Gloop machine is malfunctioning, spraying neon green sludge everywhere. A particularly grumpy cyborg, unit-88, is demanding "extra viscosity."

**Disaster:** The machine explodes, covering Unit-88 in sticky green goo. Arthur is fired on the spot by his robo-manager.

**Reaction:** Arthur retreats to the alleyway, dejected. He pulls out a hidden relic: an old, rusted French Press. He stares at it, longing for a purpose.

**Dilemma:** An old man in a trench coat (The Mentor) stumbles into the alley, pursued by drones. He shoves a data-chip (the map) into the French Press and tells Arthur, "Run, or the flavor dies with me."

**Decision:** Arthur grabs his press and runs, leaving his apron behind. He chooses the unknown over unemployment.
