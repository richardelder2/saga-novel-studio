# Story Style Guide

**Tone:**
- [ ] Dark & Gritty
- [ ] Whimsical & Light
- [ ] Academic & Dry
- [ ] Fast-paced & Action-oriented

**Perspective (POV):**
- [ ] First Person ("I")
- [ ] Third Person Limited ("He/She", close to one character)
- [ ] Third Person Omniscient ("He/She", all-knowing)

**Tense:**
- [ ] Past Tense ("He walked")
- [ ] Present Tense ("He walks")

**Formatting preferences:**
- Use standard manuscript format?
- Chapter length target: ~2000-3000 words?

**"Golden Rules":**
1.  Show, Don't Tell. (e.g., Don't say "he was angry." Describe him clenching his fists.)
2.  Deep POV. (Filter the world strictly through the character's senses.)
3.  Active Voice. (Avoid "was" and "were" where possible.)
