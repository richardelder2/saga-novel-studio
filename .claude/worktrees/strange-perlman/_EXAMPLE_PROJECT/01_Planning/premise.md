# Story Premise

**Working Title:** The Last Barista

**Genre:** Sci-Fi Comedy

**Logline:**
In a future where all food is synthetic sludge, a barista discovers a hidden cache of real coffee beans and must escape a caffeine-deprived cyborg mob to brew the perfect cup.

**Brain Dump:**
- Protagonist: Arthur, a clumsy but passionate barista.
- Antagonist: The Caffeine Collective, a group of cyborgs who run on synthetic energy drinks.
- Setting: New Seattle, a neon-drenched dystopia.
- Theme: The importance of authenticity in an artificial world.
- Scene idea: Arthur finds the beans in a cryo-pod.
- Scene idea: A chase scene on hover-scooters where Arthur uses a milk frother as a weapon.
