# Outline: The Last Barista

## Act I: The Grind

**Chapter 1: The Synthetic Sludge**
- **Action:** Arthur serves "Nutri-Gloop" to grumpy cyborgs at "Joe's Fuel Station." He dreams of real flavor.
- **Inciting Incident:** An old man (Mentor figure) gives him a cryptic map before being dragged away by the Caffeine Collective.

**Chapter 2: The Map in the Grout**
- **Action:** Arthur deciphers the map, which points to the "Old Sector," a forbidden zone.
- **Debate:** Should he go? He's cowardly, but the taste of the sludge is getting worse.

**Chapter 3: Crossing the Threshold**
- **Action:** Arthur sneaks into the Old Sector. He encounters a rogue barista bot who tests his knowledge of "The Bean."
- **Plot Point 1:** He finds the Cryo-Vault.

## Act II: The Roast

**Chapter 4: The Golden Grain**
- **Action:** Inside the vault, Arthur finds a single bag of "Ethiopian Yirgacheffe."
- **Fun & Games:** He tries to embrace the beans, realizing he has no grinder.

**Chapter 5: The Smell of Danger**
- **Action:** Ideally, he needs water. He meets classic "water pirates."
- **Conflict:** They want the beans for trade; he wants them for brewing.

**Chapter 6: The First Crack**
- **Action:** The Caffeine Collective tracks him down. The Hover-Scooter chase sequence.
- **Midpoint:** Arthur manages to grind the beans using improvised tech, but spills half of them. High stakes.

**Chapter 7: Bad Guys Close In**
- **Action:** The Collective's leader, "Commander Decaf," corners Arthur.
- **Low Point:** Arthur is captured. The beans are confiscated.

**Chapter 8: The Decaf Dungeon**
- **Action:** Arthur is in a cell. He realizes the Collective's secret: they crave the caffeine too, but their systems can't handle the purity.

## Act III: The Brew

**Chapter 9: The Breakout**
- **Action:** Arthur escapes using his barista skills (steaming a guard's sensors). He retrieves the beans.

**Chapter 10: The Perfect Temperature**
- **Action:** Arthur races to the central broadcasting tower to brew the coffee into the city's ventilation system.

**Chapter 11: The Pour Over**
- **Climax:** Arthur vs. Decaf. Arthur brews the cup. The aroma spreads. The cyborgs smell it and their aggression circuits overload with pleasure.

**Chapter 12: A New Blend**
- **Resolution:** The city wakes up. Real coffee shops open. Arthur is a hero, but he's just happy to finally have a good cup.
